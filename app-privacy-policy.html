i-ROP ASSIST Privacy Policy


Privacy Policy

Updated: 04/13/2017

The i-ROP investigative team operates i-ROP ASSIST (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the App.

While using our App, we will not ask you to provide us with any personally identifiable information, and we will not track, record, sell, share or otherwise use any data that is personal to you or the device you use the App on. The App needs the use of the camera in order to function properly. Images that are captured with the camera do not contain any geolocation or tracking data. Additionally, neither the original or modified image is kept or stored within the App after the user's immediate session is terminated.
